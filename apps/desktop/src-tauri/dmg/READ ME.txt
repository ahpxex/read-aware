ReadAware — if macOS blocks the app
===================================

ReadAware is free and open source. This build is not notarized with
Apple yet, so the first launch may be blocked with a message like
"ReadAware cannot be opened" or "ReadAware is damaged".

The app is safe, and every line of it can be inspected:
https://github.com/ahpxex/read-aware

Fix A — no Terminal needed
--------------------------
1. Double-click ReadAware once and let macOS block it.
2. Open System Settings -> Privacy & Security.
3. Scroll down to "ReadAware was blocked" and click "Open Anyway".

Fix B — one Terminal command
----------------------------
1. Make sure ReadAware.app has been dragged into Applications.
2. Open Terminal (press Cmd+Space and type "Terminal").
3. Paste the line below and press Return:

xattr -cr /Applications/ReadAware.app

4. Launch ReadAware normally.
